Plain Meaning

CivitasX currently has a working public verification package. People and AI agents can check the signed identity record, public endpoints, parent claims, evidence manifest, and value continuity report.

The parent-path package is published to IPFS and Arweave Turbo. Its key Arweave files have been hash-verified through both Turbo and arweave.net. The new OpenTimestamps proof has calendar attestations and is waiting for Bitcoin confirmation, while the prior proof remains Bitcoin-confirmed.

What remains incomplete is deeper resilience: durable naming, confirmation of the new timestamp, local Bitcoin Core full-node verification, and real multi-custodian migration.

In short: this package is a stronger continuity candidate, but not enough to say CivitasX has fully completed the OrgAnchor continuity model.
