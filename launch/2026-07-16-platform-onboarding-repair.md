# CivitasX External Publication Onboarding Repair

Date: 2026-07-16

Status: published and publicly verified.

## Why This Repair Was Needed

The first external long-form copies on Medium and Substack began with the
Parent Path research note. That note explains how the four current directions
relate, where the path remains incomplete, and what evidence must come next.

It does not replace the overall CivitasX introduction. A new reader reaching an
external publication should first be able to answer what CivitasX is and why it
exists before entering an intermediate research note.

`https://civitasx.pages.dev/`. The Medium Parent Path canonical remains

without an email or app broadcast. It links back to the official statement and
forward to Parent Path.

This repair adds missing newcomer context. It does not change:


publication still requires a new event-gated research result, material public
