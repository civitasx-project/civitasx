# OrgAnchor

OrgAnchor mainly asks:

```text
In the AI era, how can buyers and sellers use AI Agents to reduce the cost of finding, verifying, understanding, and initially judging each other?
```

OrgAnchor helps organizations publish signed identity, official presence locations, claims, evidence, and migration records.

The goal is to let people and AI Agents still discover, verify, and understand an organization at lower cost even when domains, platform accounts, websites, or infrastructure change.

OrgAnchor is not a certification authority or market ranking system.

It does not make the final judgment for outsiders. It provides a public information base that is checkable, traceable, and portable.
