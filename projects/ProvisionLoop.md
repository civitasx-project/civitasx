# ProvisionLoop

ProvisionLoop mainly asks:

```text
How can the survival freedom pursued by CivitasX become an organizational commitment to members: providing basic survival needs freely, stably, and continuously within real capability limits?
```

The real world already has continuous supply systems for survival materials.

The difference is that current systems often treat continuous human labor as a necessary input.

ProvisionLoop explores how land, resources, energy, equipment, automation systems, robots, maintenance capacity, and safety redundancy should be combined when coerced repetitive human labor gradually exits the supply loop.

The goal is to find minimal supply loops that are reasonable in input, stable in output, maintainable, repeatable, and continuously improvable.

After a loop is verified as stable enough, it can be copied, connected, and scaled, gradually approaching the goal of free and continuous basic survival provision.
