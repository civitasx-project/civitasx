# CivitasX Research

CivitasX Research publishes bounded parent-level questions when further
internal prose is no longer the main missing input.

Research notes are working maps. They must identify evidence, failure signals,
and the next route to a model, implementation, pilot, subproject, or explicit
freeze. They do not silently change CivitasX's stable values or create a fifth
project direction.
